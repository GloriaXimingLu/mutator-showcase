# FINAL CLOSING CERTIFICATE

**OF**

**WHITMORE CAPITAL MANAGEMENT LLC,**

**AS GENERAL PARTNER OF**

**WHITMORE CAPITAL PARTNERS VII, LP**

**Dated as of January 31, 2025**

This Final Closing Certificate (this "**Certificate**") is delivered by
**Whitmore Capital Management LLC**, a Delaware limited liability
company (the "**General Partner**" or "**GP**"), in its capacity as
general partner of **Whitmore Capital Partners VII, LP**, a Delaware
limited partnership (the "**Partnership**" or the "**Fund**"), in
connection with the final closing of the Fund (the "**Final Closing**"),
held on or about January 31, 2025. Capitalized terms used but not
defined in this Certificate shall have the meanings ascribed to them in
the Amended and Restated Agreement of Limited Partnership of the
Partnership, dated as of September 15, 2024, as amended by Amendment
No. 1, dated as of November 30, 2024 (as so amended, and as further
amended, restated, supplemented, or otherwise modified from time to
time, the "**Partnership Agreement**" or the "**LPA**").

## RECITALS

**A.** The Partnership held its initial closing on September 15, 2024
(the "**Initial Closing**"), at which eighteen (18) limited partners
were admitted with aggregate limited partner Capital Commitments of
\$940,000,000, together with the General Partner's Capital Commitment of
\$15,000,000, for aggregate Capital Commitments of \$955,000,000.

**B.** The Partnership held a second closing on November 30, 2024 (the
"**Second Closing**"), at which nine (9) additional limited partners
were admitted with aggregate limited partner Capital Commitments of
\$375,000,000, bringing cumulative aggregate Capital Commitments to
\$1,330,000,000.

**C.** The General Partner is consummating the Final Closing on January
31, 2025, at which six (6) additional limited partners (the "**Final
Closing Limited Partners**") are being admitted to the Partnership with
aggregate limited partner Capital Commitments of \$285,000,000.

**D.** Following the Final Closing, aggregate Capital Commitments of all
Partners (including the GP Commitment) will total \$1,615,000,000,
comprised of \$1,600,000,000 in aggregate limited partner Capital
Commitments and \$15,000,000 in GP Commitment.

**E.** Section 11.2 of the LPA sets forth certain conditions precedent
that must be satisfied or waived (to the extent permitted by Section
11.3 of the LPA) by the General Partner in connection with each Closing,
including the Final Closing. Section 9.4(d) of the LPA requires the
General Partner to certify at each Closing that each Key Person
continues to devote substantially all of his or her business time and
efforts to the Partnership and that no Key Person Event has occurred or
is reasonably expected to occur.

**F.** This Certificate is delivered by the General Partner pursuant to
Sections 11.2 and 9.4(d) of the LPA to certify the satisfaction of each
condition precedent to the Final Closing and to make the certifications
required thereunder.

### Condition 2 --- Capital Contributions (Section 11.2(2))

All Capital Contributions due at the Final Closing, including the
catch-up (equalization) contributions and equalization interest required
under Section 3.2(c) of the LPA with respect to Capital Call Notice
No. 1 (dated October 10, 2024) and Capital Call Notice No. 2 (dated
December 15, 2024), have been received in immediately available funds in
the escrow account of the Partnership maintained at Pinnacle National
Bank (account ending in 9213). The aggregate catch-up capital
contribution of the Final Closing Limited Partners is \$42,750,000
(representing 15% of aggregate Final Closing LP Capital Commitments),
together with equalization interest calculated at the rate of 8% per
annum (simple interest) in accordance with Section 3.2(c) of the LPA.
The Fund Administrator has confirmed receipt of all required funds prior
to the consummation of the Final Closing.

### Condition 3 --- AML/KYC Compliance (Section 11.2(3))

The Fund Administrator (Oakmere Fund Services LLC) has confirmed in
writing, pursuant to its email dated January 24, 2025, that satisfactory
anti-money laundering and "know your customer" documentation has been
received and verified for each of the Final Closing Limited Partners,
including (without limitation) OFAC/SDN screening results,
government-issued identification or corporate formation documents (as
applicable), beneficial ownership certifications, and politically
exposed persons (PEP) screening. No adverse findings were identified for
any Final Closing Limited Partner. The Fund Administrator has delivered
a written AML/KYC completion confirmation to the General Partner prior
to the Final Closing. The General Partner notes that this condition is
non-waivable pursuant to Section 11.3 of the LPA.

### Condition 4 --- Qualified Purchaser Status (Section 11.2(4))

Each of the Final Closing Limited Partners has represented and warranted
in its Subscription Agreement that it is a "qualified purchaser" as
defined in Section 2(a)(51) of the Investment Company Act of 1940, as
amended. The General Partner has reviewed each such representation and
has no reason to believe that any such representation is untrue, and has
determined that each such representation is consistent with the
information provided by the applicable Final Closing Limited Partner in
its Subscription Agreement and supporting documentation. The General
Partner notes that this condition is non-waivable pursuant to Section
11.3 of the LPA.

### Condition 6 --- No Material Adverse Change; Key Persons (Section 11.2(6))

No material adverse change has occurred with respect to the General
Partner, the Partnership, or the General Partner's ability to manage the
Partnership since the Second Closing on November 30, 2024. Each Key
Person (as defined in Section 9.4 of the LPA) continues to devote
substantially all of his or her business time and efforts to the
Partnership, and no Key Person Event (as defined in Section 9.4(b) of
the LPA) has occurred or is reasonably expected to occur as of the date
of the Final Closing. The General Partner's certifications with respect
to the Key Persons are set forth in Section II of this Certificate (Key
Person Certification).

### Condition 7 --- Legal Opinions (Section 11.2(7))

Fund Counsel (Bridgeford Hollis LLP) has delivered to the General
Partner and the Fund Administrator legal opinions, dated as of the date
of the Final Closing, in customary form and substance for transactions
of this type, addressing: (i) the due formation and valid existence of
the Partnership as a limited partnership under the laws of the State of
Delaware; (ii) the enforceability of the Partnership Agreement against
the Partnership and the General Partner; and (iii) such other matters as
are customary for private equity fund closings, including the limited
liability of the Limited Partners and the availability of the private
placement exemption under Regulation D under the Securities Act of 1933,
as amended.

### Condition 8 --- Updated Schedule A (Section 11.2(8))

The General Partner has caused the Fund Administrator to prepare an
updated Schedule A to the LPA reflecting the Capital Commitments of all
Partners after giving effect to the Final Closing, including the names,
Capital Commitments, dates of admission, Benefit Plan Investor status,
and qualified purchaser confirmations of all Partners. The updated
Schedule A reflects thirty-three (33) Limited Partners and the General
Partner, with aggregate Capital Commitments of \$1,615,000,000. The
updated Schedule A is attached to this Certificate as **Schedule 1** and
has been delivered to all Partners and the Fund Administrator
concurrently with the Final Closing.

### Condition 9 --- Hard Cap Compliance (Section 11.2(9))

After giving effect to the admission of all Final Closing Limited
Partners, the aggregate Capital Commitments of all Partners (including
the GP Commitment) do not exceed the Hard Cap of \$1,750,000,000 set
forth in Section 3.1(c) of the LPA. The General Partner has confirmed
that aggregate Capital Commitments total \$1,615,000,000, which is
\$135,000,000 below the Hard Cap. The General Partner has documented
this confirmation in writing.

### Condition 10 --- Representations and Warranties of the General Partner (Section 11.2(10))

The General Partner certifies that all representations and warranties of
the General Partner contained in the Partnership Agreement are true and
correct in all material respects as of the date of the Final Closing as
though made on and as of such date, except for representations and
warranties that by their terms relate to a specific date, which are true
and correct in all material respects as of such specified date. No event
has occurred since the Second Closing that would render any such
representation or warranty materially inaccurate.

## II. KEY PERSON CERTIFICATION (Section 9.4(d))

Pursuant to Section 9.4(d) of the LPA, the General Partner hereby
certifies, as of the date of the Final Closing, that:

**(a)** Each Key Person --- namely, **Marcus J. Whitmore** (Founder and
Chief Investment Officer of the General Partner) and **Priya S. Mehta**
(Managing Director of the General Partner) --- continues to devote
substantially all of his or her business time and efforts to the affairs
of the Partnership, in accordance with the requirements of Section
9.4(a) of the LPA; and

**(b)** No Key Person Event (as defined in Section 9.4(b) of the LPA)
has occurred, and no Key Person Event is reasonably expected to occur,
as of the date of the Final Closing.

The General Partner acknowledges that the Limited Partners are relying
on the continued involvement of each Key Person in making their
investment decisions and funding their Capital Commitments.

## III. ADDITIONAL CERTIFICATIONS

**(a) Target Fund Size Notification.** The General Partner acknowledges
that, following the Final Closing, aggregate Capital Commitments
(\$1,615,000,000) exceed the Target Fund Size of \$1,500,000,000 set
forth in Section 3.1(d) of the LPA by \$115,000,000. Pursuant to Section
3.1(d) of the LPA, the General Partner shall promptly notify the
Advisory Committee in writing of such excess, including the amount by
which the Target Fund Size has been exceeded and the identities and
Capital Commitments of the Partners admitted at the Final Closing, not
later than five (5) Business Days following the Final Closing.

**(b) Investment Company Act Exemption.** The Partnership continues to
rely on the exemption from registration under Section 3(c)(7) of the
Investment Company Act of 1940, as amended. Following the Final Closing,
the Partnership has thirty-four (34) beneficial owners (33 Limited
Partners plus the General Partner), each of which is a qualified
purchaser, well below the 2,000-qualified-purchaser limit applicable
under Section 3(c)(7).

**(c) SEC Registration.** The General Partner is registered as an
investment adviser with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (CRD
No. 298417; SEC File No. 801-79834) and such registration remains in
good standing as of the date hereof.

**(d) No Defaulting Partners.** No Partner has been declared a
Defaulting Partner under Section 3.3 of the LPA, and no event has
occurred that, with the passage of time or the giving of notice, would
constitute a default by any Partner under the Partnership Agreement.
