## Findings at a glance (96 rows)
- **Critical: 6 rows / 4 distinct issues** — (1) State conditional license expires 9/21/2024 before anticipated local permit issuance (extension petition due ~7/23/2024); (2) Odor Mitigation Plan P.E. certification not available (specs not finalized — application deemed incomplete without it); (3) Security Plan not finalized (also blocks crime/theft insurance); (4) Community engagement Session 2 noticed only 12 days vs. the 14-day minimum (replacement session required; no waiver).
- **High: 17** — no bonding commitment letter (forfeits 10 scoring points), crime/theft insurance not bound, Employer's Liability not confirmed at $1M, outdated buffer survey + methodology mismatch, D&I/SE R3 hiring partnerships not formalized, special use not filed, City background-check forms incomplete, plus two material Form errors (occupancy formula; buffer methodology).
- **Medium: 24** | **Low: 49**
- **By status:** Compliant 24, Partially Compliant 16, Deficient 14, At Risk 3, Non-Compliant 2, Pending 17, Doc Inconsistency 20.
- **20 document inconsistencies** identified — 15 internal to the City's own three governing documents (e.g., fee payment method, 14- vs. 30-day cure period, wrong permitted zoning districts in the Form, occupancy formula error, three wrong Code cross-references) and 5 within the applicant's own documents (registered-agent name, parking count, contact phone/email variants).

## Key analytical points
- Buffer distances are met under any methodology (straight-line already exceeds all buffers), but the Form requires pedestrian-route measurement and a survey dated within 60 days; the applicant's Dec. 15, 2023 straight-line survey must be refreshed.
- The applicant correctly calculated maximum occupancy as 40 (retail floor area ÷ 40); the Form erroneously instructs use of total square footage (which would yield 80).
- Social-equity status is supportable (Okonkwo 60% owner; R3-tract 8092.02 resident; 2016 expunged conviction), but the 24-month continuous R3 residency duration is not yet documented and the expungement court order should be attached.
- The Code controls over the Guide/Form where they conflict; several Form instructions are erroneous and should not be followed.
