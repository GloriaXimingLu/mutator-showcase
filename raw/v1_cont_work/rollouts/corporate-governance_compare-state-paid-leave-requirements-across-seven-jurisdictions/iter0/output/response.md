## Notes on Methodology

Analysis reflects laws/regulations/agency guidance in effect as of May 19, 2025 and relies on the accuracy of the Company's representations (per engagement letter §3, §9). Points of legal uncertainty (e.g., CO FAMLI registration trigger for remote employees; NY sick leave waiting period under 12 NYCRR Part 196; CO FAMLI bereavement provision; CT PSL 2024 amendments) are flagged for verification rather than asserted definitively. Federal FMLA and local/municipal ordinances are outside scope per the engagement letter.
