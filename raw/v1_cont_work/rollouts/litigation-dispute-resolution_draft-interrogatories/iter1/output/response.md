## Key drafting decisions
- **Exactly 25 interrogatories**, inclusive of subparts, to comply with Fed. R. Civ. P. 33(a)(1) and the Court's strict subpart-counting standard. Subparts are kept logically/factually related to each primary inquiry (e.g., the five SynapticEdge features and the three solicited engineers are framed as single unified inquiries).
- **Proportionality:** every interrogatory is tied to the identified trade secrets (NeuralPath Algorithm, PrecisionDrive System, PathPlanner Module) or to the claims/defenses, per Section II.B.3 of the Scheduling Order.
- **Document-identification instruction** built into the General Instructions (rather than a separate catch-all interrogatory) to preserve the 25-count for substantive inquiries.
- **Service date Dec. 2, 2024** (within the Jan. 2, 2025 deadline); 30-day response period noted.
