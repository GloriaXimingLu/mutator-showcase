        ARTICLE 5. PUBLIC INCENTIVES  
          5.1 Land Write-Down.         The sale of the Property at 50% of appraised as-is market value ($9,200,000 purchase price versus $18,400,000 appraised value) constitutes a land write-down incentive with a value of $9,200,000. The land write-down is a separate incentive and shall not be charged against the TIF Reimbursement Cap.  
          [DN — Conflict (Land write-down vs. TIF cap): The TIF Memo (Section VII) states that                 “        [i]f the City records [the land write-down] as a TIF expenditure from the Special Tax Allocation Fund, it counts against the $48 million cap,        ”                 and recommends that                 “        the development agreement clearly specify whether the land write-down is charged against the TIF cap or constitutes a separate incentive funded from another source.        ”                 The Term Sheet (Sections 5.1 and 5.6) lists the land write-down ($9,200,000) and the TIF reimbursement (up to $48,000,000) as separate incentives. Open item: confirm the City’s funding source for the land write-down.]  
          5.2 Tax Increment Financing (TIF).  
             Pledge.         The City shall pledge up to $48,000,000 (the         “    TIF Reimbursement Cap    ”    ) in TIF increment over the remaining life of the Lakemont Central TIF District to reimburse Developer for eligible TIF costs incurred in connection with the Project.  
             Eligible TIF Costs.         Eligible TIF costs shall include, without limitation: public infrastructure improvements, environmental remediation costs, parking structure construction costs, the public plaza and community center, and other eligible project costs under the TIF Act and the Lakemont Central TIF District Redevelopment Plan.  
             Pay-As-You-Go.         TIF reimbursement shall be on a pay-as-you-go basis. Developer shall front all eligible costs and shall be reimbursed annually from available TIF increment as generated by the Project and other developments within the Lakemont Central TIF District. The City shall not issue TIF bonds or notes for the Project.
