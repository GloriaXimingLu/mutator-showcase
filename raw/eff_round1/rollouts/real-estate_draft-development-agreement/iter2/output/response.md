## Key Developer Positions Taken
- **No reversionary interest** (Art. 12): substituted performance security + liquidated damages + contractual repurchase option subordinate to the lender's mortgage, to satisfy the Construction Lender's prohibition on defeasible fees while addressing the Resolution's Condition 2(h).
- **TIF**: pay-as-you-go with reimbursement priority (environmental → infrastructure → Phase IV public → other); mutual "shortfall is not a default" provision; City good-faith covenant to seek TIF extension; Land Write-Down and City infrastructure treated as separate from the $48M TIF cap.
- **Environmental**: broad, uncapped, survival-bearing City indemnity for pre-existing contamination; City bears cost overruns (25–35% contingency); NFR/remediation delays treated as Force Majeure.
- **Assignment/Change of Control**: broader permitted-transfer carve-outs (internal transfers, approved capital raises, Key Person provision, 60-day cure) to accommodate the ~$58.4M later-phase raise.
- **Community benefits**: local hiring as a good-faith goal (non-default); living wage/first-source framed as tenant-covenant obligations Developer must require, not guarantee.
- **Financing-out condition** for each Phase; expanded Developer termination rights; mutual consequential-damages waiver.
