- **Indivisible package framing:** Section IV explains the four inter-provision linkages (standstill duration ↔ triggers; par ↔ optional revolver + non-default rate; refinancing cap ↔ narrowed test + MFN; hard sweep ↔ 2L residual + ordinary-course reinvestment), and Section IX presents a gains/concessions matrix showing the aggregate balance.
