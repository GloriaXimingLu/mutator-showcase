## Key drafting decisions

- **Authoritative sources.** Where source documents conflict, the Form S-1 uses the **audited financial statements** for all financial figures and the **charter/bylaws** for governance and capital-stock terms, with bracketed `[DRAFTING NOTE]` flags at each conflict.
- **Corrected non-GAAP measure.** The CFO memo and roadshow state FY 2024 Adjusted EBITDA of $22.8M, which does not reconcile to its own add-backs (the correct sum is **$20.5M**). FY 2023 inputs in the memo also do not match the audited financials (correct Adjusted EBITDA **$3.5M**, not $7.6M). The Form S-1 presents the corrected, footing reconciliation and flags the discrepancy.
- **Accurate professional-services disclosure.** The roadshow's "high-margin" characterization is contradicted by the audited financials (PS gross margin ≈ (0.5)%). The Form S-1 describes PS accurately and adds a risk factor.
- **Governing-document names.** The CTO is referred to as "Katerina 'Kate' Litvak" (per the certificate of incorporation, bylaws, term sheet, and term loan summary); the "Litvanova" variant used in the CFO memo/equity records is flagged.
- **Related-party disclosure.** Vantara Logistics is disclosed under Item 404 (Graymont portfolio company; director Venkatesh's dual board role; Audit Committee approval and recusal).
