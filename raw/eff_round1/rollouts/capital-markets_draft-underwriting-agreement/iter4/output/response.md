# Deliverables Summary

## Task
Draft an execution-ready firm-commitment underwriting agreement for the Meridian Pulse Technologies, Inc. IPO and prepare an issues memo flagging cross-document discrepancies and open items.

## Outputs (in `output/`)
1. **`underwriting-agreement.docx`** — A complete, execution-ready firm-commitment Underwriting Agreement dated March 19, 2025, among Meridian Pulse Technologies, Inc., the three Selling Stockholders (Cascade Kestridge Ventures, LP; Northlight Growth Partners Fund II, LP; and Dr. Anand Krishnamurthy), and the Underwriters (Hargrove Securities LLC as Representative/Lead Book-Runner and Bellweather Capital Markets, Inc. as Co-Manager). The agreement contains:
   - 11 numbered sections: Company representations (§1), Selling Stockholder representations (§2), sale/delivery of shares (§3), public offering & 6% discount (§4), over-allotment option — 1,800,000 Company-only shares (§5), closing/delivery (§6), covenants including the Krishnamurthy springing lock-up extension (§7), 15 conditions to closing (§8), indemnification & contribution with statutory caps (§9), termination (§10), and miscellaneous (§11, NY governing law, notices, FINRA, expenses).
   - Signature blocks for the Company, both Underwriters, and the Selling Stockholders (via attorneys-in-fact).
   - Schedules A–C (Selling Stockholders, Underwriter allocations, lock-up parties) and Exhibits A–D (forms of Lock-Up Agreement, POA/Custody Agreement, Officers' Certificate, Secretary's Certificate).

2. **`issues-memorandum.docx`** — A privileged issues memorandum from Underwriters' counsel (Ashford & Pine LLP) flagging 21 numbered discrepancies organized by severity (6 Critical, 8 Material, 7 Drafting/Minor) plus a 32-item closing checklist. Key findings include:
   - **Critical:** Credit-facility lender named "Oakvale National Bank" in the S-1/comfort letter but "Ridgemont National Bank" in the credit-agreement summary; mandatory prepayment disclosed as ~$10M in Use of Proceeds but ~$15.24M required (and ~$15.2M stated in Risk Factors — an internal S-1 inconsistency); draft comfort-letter financials (stockholders' equity $198.4M, cash $47.3M, NTBV $4.72) do not match the S-1 capitalization/dilution figures ($101.4M, $38.4M, $2.17); greenshoe source differs (term sheet "Company and/or Selling Stockholders" vs. S-1/engagement/questionnaire "Company-only"); three conflicting descriptions of Cascade Kestridge Ventures, LP (entity name, GP, control persons, address); conflicting Northlight GP/control persons.
   - **Material:** additional credit-facility term mismatches (date, maturity, rate, change-of-control 50% vs. 35%, covenants); Krishnamurthy selling shares 500,000 vs. "up to 750,000"; shares held 8.4M vs. 8.5M; springing lock-up extension absent from form lock-up and S-1; lock-up early-release threshold and exceptions scope; MAC reference date; Cascade board-observer disclosure.
   - **Drafting/Minor:** underwriter titles, comfort-letter "Section 7(d)" cross-reference, expense-reimbursement cap, FINRA fee, VitaBand/credit-agreement judgments threshold, UT License anti-assignment interaction.

## Method
Read all 9 input documents (term sheet, S-1 excerpts, certificate of incorporation, credit-agreement summary, engagement letter, lock-up form, draft comfort letter, POA/custody agreement, selling-stockholder questionnaire), cross-referenced every material term, drafted the agreement in markdown, and generated/validated both .docx files via the docx skill (Pandoc + `validate.py`). Both files pass ZIP integrity, XML well-formedness, and relationship-consistency validation. The Underwriting Agreement was drafted to be consistent with the Registration Statement (the controlling disclosure document) wherever documents conflict, with conflicts flagged in the issues memo.
