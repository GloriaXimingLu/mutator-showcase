# Compliance Requirements Matrix — Summary of Work

## Deliverable
`output/requirements-matrix.docx` — a comprehensive compliance requirements matrix reviewing the Greenleaf Therapeutics LLC cannabis dispensary permit application package against the City of Evanston Municipal Code (Ch. 9, Art. 5) and the City's Application Checklist & Submission Guide.

## What was reviewed (8 documents)
- Evanston Municipal Code, Title 9, Ch. 9, Art. 5 (controlling ordinance)
- Application Checklist & Submission Guide
- Cannabis Dispensary Permit Application Form (v3.2, blank template)
- Greenleaf Business Plan (Executive Summary & Operations Plan)
- Community Engagement Session Documentation
- Insurance & Bonding Status Memorandum
- Summary of Key Lease Terms
- State Conditional Adult-Use Dispensing Organization License ADC-285-0147

## Document structure
1. Executive summary with status-summary table
2. Scope, methodology & authority hierarchy (Code controls over Guide/Form)
3. Documents reviewed
4. **Compliance Requirements Matrix** — 38 requirements in a color-coded landscape table (Group A: 20 application-package components; Group B: 18 operational/scoring/process requirements), each with Authority citation, Findings, Status, and Remediation Notes
5. Cross-Document Discrepancy Log — 16 conflicts among the Code, Guide, and Form
6. Applicant-internal inconsistencies to reconcile
7. Critical priority remediation actions (8, in priority order)
8. Reviewer's note & limitations

## Headline findings
- **5 critical, application-incomplete issues:** (A10) odor-mitigation PE certification not yet available (design unfinished); (A12) community-engagement Session 2 noticed only 12 days (< 14-day minimum); (A17) no bonding-capacity commitment letter obtained; (A3/B32) State conditional license expires 9/21/2024 before anticipated local permit issuance; (A9) security plan not finalized.
- **16 Code-vs-Guide-vs-Form discrepancies** the applicant must resolve, including: cure period (Code 30 days vs Guide/Form 14 days); buffer-measurement method (Code straight-line vs Form pedestrian-route); occupancy formula (Code retail-floor-area vs Form total-premises); permitted zoning districts (Code C1a/C2/oC1 vs Form C1a/C1/B2); Employer's Liability insurance (Code requires $1M/accident, omitted from Guide/Form); and others.
- **Status distribution (38 requirements):** Critical 6 · Partial 11 · Discrepancy 6 · Compliant 9 · Gap 1 · Future/ongoing 5.
