**Applicant:** Greenleaf Therapeutics LLC
**Proposed Location:** 1420 Sherman Ave., Unit B, Evanston, IL 60201 (C1a Commercial Mixed-Use district)
**State Conditional License:** ADC-285-0147 (issued 9/22/2023; expires 9/21/2024)
**Target Local Submission Date:** May 15, 2024
**Prepared for:** City of Evanston Department of Community Development — Cannabis Licensing Division
**Subject:** Compliance review of the Greenleaf Therapeutics LLC dispensary permit application package against Evanston Municipal Code Title 9, Article 5 and the City's Application Checklist & Submission Guide

---

## 1. Executive Summary

This matrix evaluates the Greenleaf Therapeutics LLC ("Greenleaf") application package against the controlling legal authority — **Evanston Municipal Code Chapter 9, Article 5** ("the Code") — and the City's **Application Checklist and Submission Guide** ("the Guide"), with reference to the City's **Cannabis Dispensary Permit Application Form, Version 3.2** ("the Form").

Greenleaf's package is, on the whole, **substantially developed and largely code-conforming on the substantive merits**: the entity is properly formed and capitalized ($1.85M, all equity); the lease provides a 7-year term with express cannabis use and landlord consent; the C1a location satisfies all buffer distances under the Code's property-line measurement standard; the business plan, financial projections, security concept, and social-equity narrative are thorough; and the community-benefit and R3 hiring commitments are correctly structured.

However, the review identifies **six critical deficiencies** that, if not cured before the May 15, 2024 submission, will either render the application incomplete or materially depress the application's competitive score:

1. **No evidence of bonding capacity** — only a verbal/preliminary inquiry with Thornfield Surety Group; no commitment letter. The Guide (Item 17) and the Form's scoring rubric require a commitment letter at submission (worth 10 of 30 Financial Viability points; zero if absent).
2. **Cannabis crime/theft insurance ($500,000) not bound** — under negotiation with Canopy Risk Underwriters, blocked on completion of the security plan.
3. **Security plan not finalized** as of the April 22, 2024 insurance memo (Pinnacle Compliance Advisors).
4. **Odor-mitigation Professional Engineer (P.E.) certification not yet available** — the business plan states the system design "will be finalized during the build-out engineering phase," but the Code (§9-5-5(B)(10)), Guide (Item 10), and Form (Q16) all require the sealed P.E. certification **at the time of submission**; absence is an automatic incompleteness trigger.
5. **State conditional license expiration timing risk** — the State license expires 9/21/2024, but the City's 120–150 day process from a 5/15/2024 submission projects permit issuance in October–November 2024. A State extension petition (due ~7/22/2024) should be filed as a precaution.
6. **Community Engagement Session 2 notice was only 12 days** (published 3/28/2024 for a 4/9/2024 session), short of the 14-day minimum in §9-5-8(B). The Code permits **no waiver** of the notice/session requirements; a non-qualifying session must be re-held.

The review also documents a series of **errors and internal inconsistencies in the City's own Form** (e.g., an occupancy formula using total rather than retail floor area; a buffer-measurement method that conflicts with the Code; incorrect zoning-district listings; wrong section citations) and **internal inconsistencies within Greenleaf's package** (registered-agent name, parking count, contact details). These are itemized in Sections 5 and 6 and should be reconciled before submission.

## 3. Methodology and Status Legend

Each requirement is traced to its source in the Code (§9-5-_), the Guide (Item _), and/or the Form (Q_), then assessed against the evidence in the application package. Status values:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Compliant** | Requirement is met; supporting evidence is present and sufficient. |
| **Partially Compliant** | Requirement is substantially addressed but contains a gap, ambiguity, or minor defect. |
| **Non-Compliant** | Requirement is not met or is in conflict with the controlling authority. |
| **Pending Submission** | Required item not yet in the package; must be produced/attached before submission. |
| **Not Applicable** | Requirement does not apply to this application. |

Items flagged **[CRITICAL]** are deficiencies that trigger automatic incompleteness, scoring loss, or a permit-jeopardizing risk if not cured. Items flagged **[FORM ERROR]** are defects in the City's Form that conflict with the Code (the Code controls).
