## Key issues identified and revised in the redline
1. **RSU valuation error (§§3(d), 3(e), 8(d)).** Draft used the outdated July 1, 2024 409A value ($10.00/sh) and called it the "most recent" valuation. The current 409A (Jan. 15, 2025) is $12.50/sh. Correct value of 5,000 RSUs is $62,500 (not $50,000); total is $537,500 (not $525,000). Corrected; added vesting-date FMV tax clarification. Memo flags the option to reduce to 4,000 RSUs to hold the total at $525,000.
2. **OWBPA/ADEA non-compliance (§§5, 16; Effective Date).** Delano is 58. Draft allowed only 14 days to consider (OWBPA requires ≥21 for an individual release; 45 + disclosures for a group/RIF) and omitted the required 7-day revocation period — either defect would jeopardize the ADEA release. Increased to 21 days; added a 7-day revocation period (new §5(g)); redefined Effective Date as the 8th day after signing; payments now due only after revocation expires.
3. **Non-compete enforceability (§9).** Removed California (void under B&P §16600). Flagged that an 18-month non-compete first imposed in a post-termination settlement is questionable under Oregon's ORS 653.295(2).
4. **Confidentiality / DTSA / whistleblower carve-outs (§6).** Added a government-agency/whistleblower carve-out (new exception (v)) and the 18 U.S.C. §1833(b) trade-secret immunity notice.
5. **"Positive" reference risk (§11).** Revised to a neutral reference (dates + title only).
6. **Perpetual non-disparagement (§7).** Limited to 5 years (mutual).
7. **Missing provisions.** Added §19 (return of company property), §20 (confidential information/IP), §21 (cooperation), §22 (governing law/venue/jury waiver); carved out the Equity Plan/RSU Grant Agreement from the integration clause (§18); narrowed "Releasees" to past and present (§1(d)).

## Strategic / litigation risks flagged in the memo
- **Replacement hire (high risk):** Greenleaf hired Raj Mehta (Senior Director, Supply Chain & Logistics) on March 20, 2025 — six days after terminating Delano — at $185,000. This contradicts the "position elimination/restructuring" rationale and strengthens Delano's retaliation (ORS 654.062(5)) and ADEA claims; informs settlement value and strategy.
- **Pending Oregon OSHA investigation** (Complaint No. OR-OSHA-2024-11872) is not resolved by this settlement; Greenleaf faces separate OSHA exposure.
- **EPLI coordination:** confirm Timberline Insurance Group (Policy TIG-EPL-2024-00391) consent, funding, limits, and conditions before executing.
- **Back-pay characterization:** $125,000 "back pay" far exceeds actual lost wages through the Effective Date (~$29,000), indicating a front-pay component.
- **Open decisions** for the GC: RSU valuation/total; OWBPA individual-vs-group characterization; whether to retain the non-compete; EPLI carrier input; replacement-hire exposure assessment.
